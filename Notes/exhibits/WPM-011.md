### 5. Design Status Update

Dr. W. Braun presented design status:

**Basic Design Package:**

| Batch | Status | Expected Approval |
|-------|--------|-------------------|
| Batch 1 - Process/Performance | Approved | Complete |
| Batch 2 Rev A - Civil/Structural | Submitted 22 April; under review | Mid-May |
| Batch 3 - Mechanical | Comments received; responses in preparation | Late May |
| Batch 4 - Electrical/I&C | Comments received 18 April; responses in preparation | Late May |

**GT1 Foundation Redesign:**

Three alternative foundation solutions have been developed:
- Option 1: Deep bored piles (25-30m) founding in competent stratum below aquifer
- Option 2: Ground improvement using vibro stone columns
- Option 3: Raft foundation with permanent pressure relief system

Technical Coordination Meeting (TCM-002) rescheduled to 29 April to present and evaluate options.

D. Kostić reiterated BEU's preference for a solution that does not require permanent dewatering or ongoing maintenance.

**Action:** WPM-011/02 - A. Kováč to circulate GT1 foundation options brief before TCM-002 | Due: 26 April 2019
