### 2. Basic Design Approval Achievement

Dr. H. Weber formally announced that **Basic Design Approval has been achieved as of 12 June 2019**.

The GT1 Foundation design was approved this morning (DPI/SPCC/2019/0008). This completes all Basic Design packages:
- Batch 1: Civil/Structural (General)
- Batch 2: Civil/Structural (Foundations)
- Batch 3: Mechanical
- Batch 4: Electrical/I&C
- GT1 Foundation (special package)

I. Petrović congratulated all parties on achieving this milestone and thanked the Design Task Force for the expedited reviews.

M. Horák acknowledged the achievement but noted it is 58 days after the contractual milestone. SPCC maintains its position that a significant portion of this delay is attributable to Employer risk events.

Dr. H. Weber noted for the record that the reasons for the delay are disputed and will be addressed through the claims process.
