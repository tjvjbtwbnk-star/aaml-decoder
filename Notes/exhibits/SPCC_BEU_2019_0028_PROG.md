In accordance with Sub-Clause 4.21 of the Conditions of Contract, we hereby submit our Monthly Progress Report for the period 1-31 August 2019.

**1. Executive Summary**

August 2019 has been a challenging but productive month. Despite encountering unforeseen ground conditions at the HRSG area (resulting in VO-004), significant progress has been achieved across all work fronts. The Cooling Tower foundations are now complete, GT1 foundation slab has been poured, and structural steel erection has commenced.

**Key Achievements:**
- Cooling Tower foundations 100% complete (21 August)
- GT1 pile cap complete (22 August)
- GT1 foundation slab poured (28 August)
- HRSG additional piling complete (25 August)
- Structural steel erection commenced (26 August)
- Grid connection data received (26 August)
- VO-003 and VO-004 agreements reached
