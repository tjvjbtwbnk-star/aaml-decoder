**2. Scope of Inspection**

The inspection covered all foundation areas identified in the Contract as comprising the Foundation Completion milestone:

| Foundation Area | Drawing Reference | Status |
|-----------------|-------------------|--------|
| Administration Building | FDN-ADMIN-001 to 003 | Complete |
| Gas Turbine 1 | FDN-GT1-001 to 008 | Complete |

| Cooling Tower | FDN-CT-001 to 008 | Complete |

**3. Inspection Findings**

**3.1 Administration Building**
- Foundations complete and in accordance with approved drawings
- No defects observed
- As-built survey confirms dimensional compliance

**3.2 Gas Turbine 1**
- Bored piles (28 No.): Installation records reviewed – compliant
- Pile cap: Complete with construction joints prepared as specified
- Foundation slab: Complete – surface finish satisfactory
- Embedded items: Installed and surveyed – within tolerance
- No defects observed

**3.3 HRSG Area**
- Standard foundations (Pours 1-7, 11-12): Complete
- Additional piled foundations (Pours 8-10): Complete
- Surface finish: Satisfactory
- Minor observation: Hairline shrinkage crack noted at Pour 4 (cosmetic only, no structural significance)

**3.4 Cooling Tower**
- All foundations complete
- No defects observed
- Embedded anchor bolts surveyed – within tolerance

**6. Certification**

Having completed my inspection and reviewed the relevant documentation, I hereby certify that:

**7. Date of Completion**

The date of Foundation Completion is certified as **7 September 2019**.

The contractual date for Foundation Completion was 1 September 2019. The certified date of completion is 7 September 2019, representing a delay of **6 calendar days**.

A separate determination regarding the attribution of this delay and any resulting consequences under the Contract will be issued in due course.
