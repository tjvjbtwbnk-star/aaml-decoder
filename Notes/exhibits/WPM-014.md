### 2. Remediation Progress

A. Kováč presented remediation status:

**Week 1 Progress (15-22 May):**
- Excavation commenced 15 May as planned
- Cumulative excavation: 1,850 m³ of estimated 5,400 m³ (34%)
- Contaminated soil removed to Szohôd facility: 2,775 tonnes (68 truck loads)
- Groundwater extraction: 420 m³/day average
- All groundwater treated and discharged within permit limits

**Quality:**
- Air monitoring: All readings well below action levels
- No odour complaints
- TPH concentrations in excavated material: 1,200 - 3,800 mg/kg (as expected)

**Issues:**
- Minor delay on Day 3 due to pump failure (4 hours) - resolved
- Slightly higher water inflow than estimated - additional pump deployed

**Forecast:**
- Excavation complete: 10 June (on target)
- Verification sampling: 11-14 June
- Backfilling: 17-28 June
- Overall completion: 28 June (on target)

D. Kostić confirmed BEU is satisfied with progress. He noted the daily reports are informative and requested they continue.

M. Santos advised she would conduct a detailed inspection on 24 May and witness verification sampling when scheduled.
