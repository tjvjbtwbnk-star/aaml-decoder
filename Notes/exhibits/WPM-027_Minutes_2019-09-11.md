### 3. Foundation Completion – Certification and Determination

**3.1 Certification**

Dr. Weber (DPI) confirmed that Foundation Completion was certified on 9 September 2019, with completion dated as 7 September 2019.

The punch list items have been completed as of 10 September.

**3.2 Delay Determination**

Dr. Weber summarised the determination issued on 10 September (DPI/SPCC/2019/0010-DET):
- Total delay: 6 days
- Employer risk (VO-004): 5 days
- Contractor risk: 1 day
- Delay damages: EUR 25,000

J. Procházka (SPCC) stated that SPCC does not agree with the determination and will be issuing a notice of dissatisfaction under Sub-Clause 3.5. SPCC maintains that all delay is attributable to Employer risk events.

I. Petrović (BEU) stated that BEU accepts the Engineer's determination and expects the delay damages to be deducted from the next payment certificate.

Dr. Weber noted the Contractor's position and confirmed that a notice of dissatisfaction, if issued, will be dealt with in accordance with the Contract.
