### 2. Safety Report – Including Incident Discussion

P. Horváth reported:

**Statistics (week to 17 Sep):**
- Man-hours: 12,800
- LTIs: 0
- Near-misses: 2

**Incident Update:**
P. Horváth informed the meeting that a lifting incident occurred yesterday (18 September) at approximately 14:35. Two workers sustained minor injuries. A detailed report is being prepared.

M. Horák confirmed that lifting operations have been suspended pending investigation and will resume once corrective actions are implemented.

I. Petrović (BEU) expressed concern and requested a full briefing once the investigation is complete. M. Horák confirmed this will be provided.

**Note:** Due to the incident, the scheduled Safety Committee Meeting (SCM-001) has been postponed from today to allow for incident investigation. It will be rescheduled for next week.
