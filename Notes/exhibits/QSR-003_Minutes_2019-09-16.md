## Minutes

### 1. Welcome and Introduction

Dr. Vladić welcomed attendees to the third Quarterly Senior Review. He noted that Q3 2019 has been a challenging quarter with several significant events:
- VO-003 (Cooling System) – major variation agreed
- VO-004 (Additional Piling) – unforeseen ground conditions
- Foundation Completion milestone – 6 days late, with delay determination
- Grid data delay – claim disputed

He emphasised the importance of the Parties working together constructively to deliver this important national infrastructure project.
