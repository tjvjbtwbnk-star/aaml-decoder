### 2. Description of Non-Conformance

The 28-day compressive strength test result for concrete cube sample FDN-GT1-005, taken from the western area of the GT1 foundation slab during the pour on 28 August 2019, has failed to meet the specified minimum strength.

| Parameter | Specified | Actual | Variance |
|-----------|-----------|--------|----------|
| 28-day compressive strength | ≥40 MPa | 36 MPa | -10% |

### 3. Specification Reference

| Document | Clause | Requirement |

| Mix Design Approval | MDA-015 | Approved mix design for GT1 foundations |
| BS EN 206 | Table F.1 | Conformity criteria for compressive strength |

## PART C – ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS (PRELIMINARY)

### 9. Potential Causes

| No. | Potential Cause | Likelihood | Evidence |
|-----|-----------------|------------|----------|
| 1 | Extended placement time | High | Pour log shows 11 hours vs 8 planned; west area poured last (15:00-17:30) |
| 2 | Water addition to improve workability | Medium | Not recorded but cannot be excluded |
| 3 | Inadequate compaction | Medium | Edge location – limited vibrator access |
| 4 | Concrete batch variation | Low | Batch tickets show compliance |
| 5 | Cube sampling/curing error | Low | Witnessed by DPI; standard procedures followed |

### 10. Contributing Factors

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Pump blockages | Two blockages caused delays totalling 70 minutes |
| Traffic delays | Concrete truck deliveries delayed in afternoon |
| Pour duration | 11 hours vs 8 planned – increased risk of initial set |
| Late afternoon timing | Higher ambient temperature, faster moisture loss |

### 11. Preliminary Root Cause

Based on preliminary investigation, the most likely root cause is:

**Extended concrete placement time in the western area, resulting in partial initial set before placement and/or inadequate workability leading to poor compaction.**

*Final root cause determination pending core test results.*
