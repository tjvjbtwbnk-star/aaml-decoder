**2. REVIEW OUTCOME**

The Engineer has completed review of the above documents and hereby grants approval, with comments, for Batch 1 Rev A of the Basic Design submission.

This approval is issued in accordance with Sub-Clause 5.2.2 with the following classification:

**APPROVED WITH COMMENTS (Code B)**

The Contractor is authorised to proceed with detailed design development based on this package, subject to incorporation of the comments listed in the attached schedule.

**3. OUTSTANDING COMMENTS**

The attached Comment Resolution Schedule identifies 8 minor comments (all Category C - Observations) which should be incorporated into subsequent design development. These do not prevent approval at this stage but should be addressed in the Detailed Design phase.

Key observations include:

(a) **BCCPP-PR-002:** Ambient temperature basis should be clearly stated on all drawings (currently only in Design Basis document)

(b) **BCCPP-PR-005:** Performance test procedure outline should reference specific ISO standards for combined cycle testing

(c) **BCCPP-PR-007:** Cooling water chemistry limits should be coordinated with cooling system vendor requirements (to be confirmed during detailed design)

**4. CONDITIONS**

This approval is granted subject to the following conditions:

4.1 The design basis parameters established in BCCPP-PR-001 Rev A shall be maintained throughout detailed design unless formally varied.

4.2 Any changes to the guaranteed performance parameters in BCCPP-PR-005 shall require separate approval.

4.3 The comments identified in the attached schedule shall be addressed in subsequent submissions.

**5. EMPLOYER'S CONSENT**

In accordance with Sub-Clause 5.2.2, this approval has been coordinated with the Employer. The Employer has confirmed no objection to this approval.
