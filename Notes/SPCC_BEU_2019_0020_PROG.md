**Subject: Monthly Progress Report – June 2019**

**Reference: Contract No. BEU-BCCPP-2018-001, Sub-Clause 4.21**

Dear Mrs. Petrović,

In accordance with Sub-Clause 4.21 of the Conditions of Contract, we hereby submit our Monthly Progress Report for the period 1-30 June 2019.

**1. Executive Summary**

June 2019 has been a month of significant achievement for the Project. The key milestone of Basic Design Approval was achieved on 12 June 2019, following the successful approval of Batch 4 (Electrical/I&C) on 7 June and the GT1 Foundation package on 12 June. We are pleased to report that the programme has recovered to baseline with SPI at 1.00 and 15 days of float restored.

**2. Design Progress**

- Basic Design: 100% complete (approved 12 June 2019)
- Detailed Design: 31% complete against 35% planned
- Design Task Force meetings continue twice weekly with productive outcomes
- Cooling system detailed design on hold pending resolution of DE-003

**3. Procurement Progress**

- GT1 FAT scheduled for 15-19 July 2019 at TurboMach facility, Zürich
- HRSG components on track for Q4 2019 delivery
- Main transformer manufacturing progressing at Sildavo Electrical Works
- All Batch 3 and 4 procurement packages issued

**4. Construction Progress**

4.1 *Earthworks*
- Overall: 92% complete
- HRSG area grading complete
- GT1 area backfilling completed 24 June following remediation

4.2 *Piling*
- Non-GT1 areas: 100% complete (158 piles)
- GT1 bored piling: Commenced 1 July 2019 (3 of 28 piles complete as of reporting date)

4.3 *Foundations*
- Admin Building: 67% complete (4 of 6 pours)
- HRSG Area: 25% complete (3 of 12 pours)
- Cooling Tower: 13% complete (1 of 8 pours)

**5. Health, Safety & Environment**

- Man-hours worked in June: 48,250
- LTI: 0
- Near misses reported: 3 (all closed out)
- Environmental incidents: 0

**6. Programme Status**

- Schedule Performance Index (SPI): 1.00
- Float: 15 days
- Critical Path: GT1 piling → GT1 foundation → GT1 installation
- Note: DE-002 and DE-003 EOT claims pending determination not included in current baseline

**7. Commercial Status**

- Current Contract Price: EUR 849,595,000 (inclusive of VO-001 and VO-002)
- Pending Variations: VO-003 (submitted 3 July 2019)
- Active Claims: DE-002 (68 days), DE-003 (85 days EOT, EUR 22,300,000)

**8. Key Activities for July 2019**

- Complete GT1 bored piling (target: 26 July)
- Continue foundation works in HRSG and Cooling Tower areas
- GT1 FAT at TurboMach (15-19 July)
- Progress detailed design to 45%
- DE-002 response due from Engineer (19 July)

**9. Key Issues and Concerns**

- DE-003 resolution required to progress cooling system detailed design
- GT1 piling productivity to be monitored given challenging ground conditions
- Hot weather conditions affecting concrete curing times

The detailed progress report, including programme update, S-curves, and photographic record, is enclosed.

We remain committed to the successful delivery of this Project and look forward to discussing this report at the next Project Management Meeting.
