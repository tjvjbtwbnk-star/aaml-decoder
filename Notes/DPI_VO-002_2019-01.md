Dear Ms. Petrović,

**1. INTRODUCTION**

In accordance with Sub-Clause 12.3 of the Conditions of Contract, the Engineer has evaluated the Contractor's Variation Request for contaminated soil disposal works (VO-002) submitted on 17 May 2019.

This evaluation is submitted to the Employer for consideration and approval.

**2. BACKGROUND**

2.1 Contaminated soil was discovered during ground investigation in March-April 2019.

2.2 The contamination (petroleum hydrocarbons) exceeded regulatory limits and required remediation before GT1 foundation construction could proceed.

2.3 The Employer issued an instruction (BEU/SPCC/2019/0009-TEC dated 25 April 2019) directing the Contractor to undertake remediation works.

2.4 Remediation was substantially completed on 11 June 2019 with all verification samples passing.

**5. ENGINEER'S RECOMMENDATION**

5.1 The Engineer recommends approval of Variation Order VO-002 in the amount of:

**EUR 1,850,000** (One Million Eight Hundred Fifty Thousand Euros)

5.2 This amount includes:
- Assessed value: EUR 1,844,339
- Rounding and contingency: EUR 5,661

5.3 The recommended amount exceeds the Contractor's claim due to:
- Actual quantities exceeding estimates (volume, tonnage, loads)
- Slightly extended duration of works
