### 3. GT1 FAT Outcome

Dr. Braun presented the GT1 Factory Acceptance Test results from TurboMach, Zürich (15-19 July 2019):

**Summary:**
- All witness points successfully completed
- No major non-conformances identified
- Four minor punch list items recorded (cosmetic only)
- FAT Certificate signed 19 July 2019

**Key Achievements:**
- Rotor balance test: Within specification
- Control system demonstration: All functions verified
- Performance projections: Confirmed per guaranteed values
- Documentation package: Complete

**Punch List Items:**
1. Minor paint damage on inlet plenum (to be rectified prior to shipping)
2. Labelling correction on auxiliary panel
3. Documentation index update required
4. Torque marking on two flange bolts

D. Kostić (BEU) confirmed BEU is satisfied with the FAT outcome and commended the joint team effort. GT1 shipping scheduled for late October 2019 for March 2020 site delivery.
