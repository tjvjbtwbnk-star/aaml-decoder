In accordance with Sub-Clause 4.21 of the Conditions of Contract, we hereby submit our Monthly Progress Report for the period 1-30 September 2019.

**1. Executive Summary**

September 2019 marked the completion of Q3 and saw significant progress despite ongoing challenges. Foundation Completion was certified, structural steel erection advanced substantially, and detailed design reached the 55% milestone. However, the month also saw a safety incident and the discovery of a potential quality issue with the GT1 foundation slab.

**Key Achievements:**
- Foundation Completion certified (7 September)
- Admin Building steel erection: 55% → 78%
- HRSG steel erection commenced
- Detailed design reached 55%
- GT1 shipping confirmed for 15 October

**Key Concerns:**
- Crane incident on 18 September (1 MTC)
- GT1 foundation concrete investigation underway
- Claims settlement discussions ongoing
