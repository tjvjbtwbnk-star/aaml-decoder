### 7. Cooling System Instruction

D. Kostić presented the cooling system instruction:

**Background:**
- New Ministry of Environment regulations on thermal discharge
- Issued April 2019, effective January 2020
- Impact on BCCPP cooling system design

**BEU Instruction:**
- Letter BEU/SPCC/2019/0013-TEC issued 5 June
- Instructs SPCC to redesign cooling system to hybrid (wet/dry) configuration
- Constitutes Variation under Sub-Clause 13.1

**Key Requirements:**
- Maximum thermal discharge to River Sava: 3°C above ambient (reduced from 5°C)
- Water consumption reduced by 40%
- Hybrid cooling tower required

**Impact (Preliminary):**
- Cost impact: EUR 15-20 million (BEU estimate)
- Programme impact: Under assessment by SPCC

M. Horák acknowledged receipt of the instruction. He noted:
- SPCC will assess the impact and respond within 28 days
- This is a significant change requiring careful evaluation
- Extension of Time and cost entitlement to be claimed

Dr. W. Braun confirmed the design team is already reviewing the instruction.

**Action:** WPM-016/02 - SPCC to submit response to cooling system instruction | Due: 3 July 2019
