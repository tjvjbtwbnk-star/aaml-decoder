Dear Mr. Králík,

## RE: BORDURIA COMBINED CYCLE POWER PLANT PROJECT – ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF COMMENCEMENT AND INITIAL COORDINATION

Thank you for your letter ref. SPCC/BEU/2019/0001-GEN dated 15 January 2019 confirming the Commencement Date of 15 January 2019.

BEU acknowledges receipt of this notice and confirms its readiness to support SPCC's mobilisation activities in full accordance with the Contract requirements. We are pleased to see the Project officially commence and share your optimism regarding the collaborative working relationship that will be essential to its success.

Regarding Site possession, BEU confirms that arrangements are progressing to provide access to the Site on 1 February 2019 as planned. Our Site Representative, Mr. Branko Simić, will coordinate with your Construction Manager regarding the formal handover procedures and any initial access requirements you may have.

We concur with your proposal for a kick-off meeting during the week of 21 January 2019. Ms. Ivana Petrović, our Project Manager, will liaise with Mr. Horák to finalize arrangements. We suggest the meeting be held at BEU's offices in Szohôd to facilitate participation by our senior management team.

BEU looks forward to working with SPCC and the Engineer to deliver this strategically important project on schedule and to the required performance standards. Should you require any clarification or assistance during the mobilisation phase, please do not hesitate to contact Ms. Petrović or myself.

Yours sincerely,

**Dr. Miloš Vladić**  
Project Director  
Borduria Energy Utilities S.A.

cc: Dr. Heinrich Weber, Engineer's Representative (DPI)
