**Subject: Approval of Quality Management Plan (TR-005)**

**Reference: Contract No. BEU-BCCPP-2018-001; Sub-Clause 4.9 (Quality Assurance)**

Dear Mr. Malý,

**1. DOCUMENTS REVIEWED**

We refer to your submission of the Quality Management Plan (Technical Report TR-005) dated 21 May 2019, submitted for approval in accordance with Sub-Clause 4.9 of the Contract.

The document has been reviewed by the Employer's Technical Manager and is considered to meet the requirements of the Contract and the Employer's Requirements.

**2. APPROVAL**

The Employer hereby **approves** the Quality Management Plan (TR-005 Rev 0) dated 21 May 2019.

This approval is granted subject to the following comments being addressed in the next revision:

**2.1 Minor Comments (for incorporation):**

(a) **Section 4.1 (Organisation Chart):** Please update to include the interface with the Employer's Quality Manager (Ms. Milica Todorović), who joins the project on 1 June 2019.

(b) **Section 5.1 (Procedure Index):** Procedure QP-015 (Commissioning Quality Control) should be added to cover the commissioning phase quality requirements.

(c) **Section 6.3 (Hold Points):** Please add a Hold Point for "First concrete pour for each foundation type" to ensure the Employer/Engineer has opportunity to witness initial production.

(d) **Section 7.1 (Approved Supplier List):** Please add any Tier 2 suppliers for critical items (e.g., valve manufacturers, instrument suppliers) as they are identified.

**2.2 Observations:**

(e) The NCR closure target of 30 days is noted. BEU expects that safety-critical and quality-critical NCRs will be prioritised for faster resolution.

(f) The quarterly management review frequency is acceptable, but BEU reserves the right to request more frequent reviews if quality issues emerge.

**3. INSPECTION AND TEST PLANS**

3.1 We note that ITP-CV-001 (Piling Works) has been submitted separately for approval. This is currently under review and a response will be issued within 7 days.

3.2 Please ensure that all ITPs are submitted at least 14 days before the relevant work commences, as required by the Contract.

**4. EMPLOYER'S QUALITY MANAGER**

4.1 Ms. Milica Todorović will join the Employer's project team as Quality Manager on 1 June 2019.

4.2 Ms. Todorović will be the primary point of contact for quality matters and will coordinate the Employer's quality surveillance activities.

4.3 Please arrange for a Quality coordination meeting within the first week of June to establish working arrangements.

**5. QUALITY SURVEILLANCE**

5.1 The Employer intends to conduct regular quality surveillance in accordance with the Contract.

5.2 Advance notice of Hold Point activities shall be provided as follows:
- 48 hours minimum for on-site activities
- 5 working days for factory inspections

5.3 The Employer reserves the right to conduct unannounced quality audits with 24 hours notice.

**6. EFFECTIVE DATE**

This approval is effective from the date of this letter. The Contractor shall implement the Quality Management Plan immediately and ensure all personnel are briefed on its requirements.

We look forward to working collaboratively to ensure the highest quality standards are achieved on this project.

Yours sincerely,
